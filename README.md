## ✨ Key Features

### 💬 **Smart Conversations**
- **🔤 Text Chat**: Type your thoughts and receive thoughtful, empathetic responses
- **🎤 Voice Chat**: Speak naturally and hear AI responses with customizable voices
- **🧠 Context Memory**: AI remembers your conversation history for personalized support
- **🌍 Multi-Language**: Available in multiple languages for global accessibility

### 🎨 **Personalization**
- **🌙 Theme Options**: Beautiful light and dark modes with multiple color schemes
- **🔊 Voice Customization**: Adjust speech rate, pitch, volume, and voice selection
- **📱 Adaptive Interface**: Clean, intuitive design that adapts to your preferences
- **⚙️ Settings Control**: Fine-tune every aspect of your experience

### 📊 **Progress Tracking**
- **📚 Chat History**: Access and review all your previous conversations
- **📈 Usage Analytics**: Track patterns and insights about your mental health journey
- **🎯 Progress Dashboard**: Visualize your activity and growth over time
- **📋 Conversation Management**: Archive, search, and organize your chats

### 🔒 **Privacy & Security**
- **🏠 Local Storage**: All conversations stored securely on your device
- **🚫 No Data Sharing**: Your privacy is completely protected
- **🗑️ Full Control**: Delete or archive conversations anytime you want

## 📖 How to Use

### 💬 **Getting Started with Text Chat**

<details>
<summary>Click to expand detailed instructions</summary>

1. **🚀 Launch** the AI Psychologist app
2. **👀 See** the main chat interface with a clean, welcoming design
3. **⌨️ Type** your message in the text field at the bottom
4. **📤 Tap** the send button to share your thoughts
5. **💭 Receive** thoughtful, empathetic AI responses instantly
6. **🔄 Continue** the conversation - the AI remembers your context!

</details>

### 🎤 **Using Voice Chat**

<details>
<summary>Click to expand detailed instructions</summary>

1. **📱 Navigate** to Voice Chat from the side menu
2. **🎙️ Tap** the microphone button when ready to speak
3. **🗣️ Speak** naturally - share whatever's on your mind
4. **👂 Listen** to AI responses with customizable voice settings
5. **⚙️ Adjust** voice preferences (speed, pitch, volume) in settings
6. **🔄 Continue** the voice conversation as long as you need

</details>

### 📊 **Tracking Your Progress**

<details>
<summary>Click to expand detailed instructions</summary>

1. **📈 Visit** your personal Dashboard from the menu
2. **👀 View** detailed statistics about your app usage
3. **📚 Browse** your complete chat history and conversations
4. **🗂️ Organize** conversations by archiving or deleting them
5. **📊 Monitor** patterns in your mental health discussions
6. **🎯 Track** your progress and wellness journey over time

</details>

### 🎨 **Customizing Your Experience**

<details>
<summary>Click to expand detailed instructions</summary>

**Themes & Appearance:**
- Go to ⚙️ Settings → 🎨 Appearance
- Choose between 🌞 Light or 🌙 Dark mode
- Select your favorite color scheme (Orange, Blue, Green, etc.)

**Voice Settings:**
- Navigate to 🎤 Voice Chat → ⚙️ Voice Settings
- Adjust 🔊 volume, ⚡ speed, and 🎵 pitch sliders
- Choose your preferred 🗣️ voice and 🌍 language
- Tap "🧪 Test Voice" to preview your settings

</details>

## 🛠️ For Developers

### 📋 **Prerequisites**
```bash
✅ Flutter SDK 3.0.0+
✅ Dart SDK 2.17.0+
✅ Android Studio or VS Code
✅ Git installed on your system
```

### 🚀 **Installation & Setup**

```bash
# 1️⃣ Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/Omara-25/Ai_psychologist-App.git

# 2️⃣ Navigate to project directory
cd Ai_psychologist-App

# 3️⃣ Install all dependencies
flutter pub get

# 4️⃣ Run the app on your device/emulator
flutter run
```

### 🏗️ **Project Structure**
```
lib/
├── 📱 main.dart              # App entry point
├── 🖼️ screens/              # All app screens
├── 🧩 widgets/              # Reusable UI components
├── 💾 models/               # Data models
├── 🔧 services/             # Business logic & APIs
├── 🎨 themes/               # App themes & styling
└── 🛠️ utils/               # Helper functions
```

### 🧰 **Built With**
- **Flutter** - Cross-platform mobile framework
- **Dart** - Programming language
- **SQLite** - Local database for chat storage
- **Provider** - State management
- **TTS/STT** - Text-to-Speech & Speech-to-Text
